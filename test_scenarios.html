Scenario 1: Inventory Management (Receive & Transfer)

Objective: Verify stock entry into the Main Store and subsequent transfer to a sales point (Bar).

# Action Expected Result Pass/Fail
1 Login as Admin or Manager. Dashboard loads successfully.
2 Navigate to Receive Stock. Receive Stock form appears with "Location" dropdown.
3 Select Location: "Main Warehouse".
Select Product: "Coca Cola".
Enter Qty: 100.
Click Confirm.		 Success message appears: "Successfully received 100 units".
4 Navigate to Inventory to verify. "Coca Cola" in "Main Warehouse" shows 100 units.
5 Navigate to Transfers -> New Transfer. Transfer form loads.
6 From: "Main Warehouse"
To: "Main Bar"
Item: "Coca Cola"
Qty: 50.
Click Transfer.		 System confirms transfer. Stock deducts from Warehouse immediately.
7 Navigate to Inventory (Filter by "Main Bar"). "Main Bar" stock for "Coca Cola" increased by 50.

Scenario 2: Standard Sale & Kitchen Workflow (KDS)

Objective: Process a food order, track it through the kitchen, and complete collection.

# Action Expected Result Pass/Fail
1 Login as Cashier/Waiter. Select Location "Main Bar". POS Interface loads. Product grid visible.
2 Add "Beef Burger" (Food) and "Coca Cola" (Drink) to cart. Cart updates. Total calculation is correct.
3 Click Charge -> Single Pay -> Cash. Sale completes. Receipt modal/print triggers. Cart clears.
4 Simulate Kitchen: Open a new browser/tab.
Login as Chef -> Go to KDS.		 "Beef Burger" appears on KDS screen (Status: Pending). "Coca Cola" does NOT appear (Drinks auto-complete).
5 Chef clicks Start Cooking, then Mark Ready. Item turns Green/Disappears from Active view.
6 Back to POS: Click Pickup button (Top Menu). Pickup Monitor shows Order #XX as "READY".
7 Click Mark Collected on the monitor. Order is removed from Pickup list. Fulfillment status updated.

Scenario 3: Advanced Billing (Splitting & Tabs)

Objective: Handle complex payments involving split bills and running tabs.

# Action Expected Result Pass/Fail
1 On POS, add 2x Burgers and 2x Cokes. Cart Total reflects 4 items.
2 Click Split button. Split Modal opens with "Unassigned Items" pool.
3 Click + to add "Guest 2".
Drag 1 Burger & 1 Coke to "Guest 2".		 Items move from pool to Guest 2 column. Totals recalculate.
4 Set Guest 1 to Cash. Set Guest 2 to Pending (Tab).
Click Finalize & Pay.		 System processes 2 transactions. Guest 1 receipt prints. Guest 2 is saved to Tab.
5 Click Tabs button. Tabs list shows a new tab (Guest 2) with unpaid balance.
6 Select the tab -> Click Pay / Add Items. Cart restores with Guest 2's items.
7 Add a "Water". Click Add to Tab -> Select existing tab. Item added to existing tab without payment. Cart clears.
8 Recall Tab again. Click Charge -> Pay Full Balance. Transaction completes. Tab is marked "PAID" and closed.

Scenario 4: Refunds & Shift End

Objective: Process a return and close the daily register.

# Action Expected Result Pass/Fail
1 Go to Reports / Receipt History. List of recent sales appears.
2 Find a recent sale. Click Refund. Refund modal appears.
3 Enter Reason: "Customer Complaint".
Enter Manager Password.		 Refund is approved. Stock is returned to inventory (if applicable). Cash deducted.
4 Return to POS. Click X-Read. Shift report shows Total Sales minus Refunds.
5 Click End Shift. End Shift modal appears asking for Closing Cash.
6 Enter correct cash amount. Click Close Register. Shift closes. System logs out or returns to Dashboard.
