Odelia POS v2.0 | Staff Training Guide

Step-by-step guides for handling real-world operations in Odelia POS.

Scenario 1: Processing a Split Payment

A customer's bill is ZMW 500. They want to pay ZMW 200 in Cash and the remaining ZMW 300 via Mobile Money.

1
Ring up the items so the total shows ZMW 500. Click the green PAY button.
2
In the payment modal, select Cash. Change the "Tendered Amount" from 500 to 200, then click Submit.
3
The POS will remain open, showing a remaining balance of ZMW 300.
4
Click PAY again, select Mobile Money, leave the amount at 300, and hit Submit to close the ticket and print the receipt.

Scenario 2: Transferring a Table

A group at Table 4 wants to move outside to the Patio (Table 12).

1
Select Table 4 from your active POS tabs to load their order.
2
Click the Transfer Table button located near the order total.
3
Select Table 12 from the dropdown menu and confirm. The tab is instantly relocated without interrupting the kitchen.

Scenario 3: End of Day (Z-Read)

Your shift is over, and you need to close the till and hand over the cash.

1
From the POS screen, click your name in the top right and select End Shift.
2
Count the physical cash in your drawer (including your starting float) and type that number into the Actual Cash Declared box.
3
Click Confirm & Close Shift.
4
The system will generate your Z-Read report, highlighting any overages or shortages, and securely archive it for management.
