takePOS | System User Manual

1. System Access & Authentication

  • Login: Enter your assigned username and password. The system utilizes secure, enterprise-grade encryption for all sessions.
  • Session Management: Depending on your assigned role, you will be automatically redirected to the Cashier POS, Kitchen KDS, or Admin Dashboard upon logging in.
  • Global Location Switcher: If you have managerial access, click your current location in the top-right navigation bar to instantly switch your active workstation without logging out.

2. Shift Management

  • Opening a Shift: Upon accessing the POS screen, cashiers must enter their starting float (Opening Cash) to begin ringing up sales.
  • Real-Time Shift Status: Managers can click on any active shift from the Dashboard to see expected cash drawer totals, total revenue, and a drill-down of every transaction.
  • Closing a Shift (Z-Read): Cashiers end their shift by declaring their physical cash. The system calculates overages or shortages and permanently archives the Z-Read report.

3. Point of Sale (POS) Operations

  • Adding Items: Click products from the menu grid or use an attached barcode scanner to add items to the current tab.
  • Partial Payments & Split Tenders: Click "Pay" and enter an amount lower than the total. The system will track the remaining balance. Multiple payment methods (e.g., Cash + Mobile Money) can be used on a single ticket.
  • Table Management: Assign orders to specific tables. Use the "Transfer Table" button to move a customer's tab seamlessly to another seating area.
  • Voids & Refunds: Only authorized roles can void an item from a pending tab or process a refund for a previously closed sale.

4. Inventory & Stock Control

  • Receive Stock: Add new inventory via the Purchase Order screen. The system automatically updates average cost metrics based on the new purchasing price.
  • Stock Transfers: Move inventory physically between locations (e.g., from the Main Warehouse to a Branch location).
  • Recipe Consumption: Selling a composite item (like a Burger) automatically deducts the exact raw ingredients (buns, beef patties) mapped in the Menu Builder.
