End-to-End User Acceptance Test (UAT)

Objective: Validate data flow from Inventory (Back Office) to Kitchen (Production) to Customer (Collection).

Pre-Requisites

  • Admin/Manager PC logged in.
  • POS Terminal (or Cashier login) ready.
  • Kitchen Display (Chef login) ready.

Phase 1: The Supply Chain (Inventory)

Goal: Ensure the kitchen has stock to sell.

  1. Create Test Item:
    • Name: Test Burger | Price: 150.00 | Category: Food.
  2. Receive Stock (Warehouse):
    • Add 100 units to Main Warehouse.
    • Verify: Stock count reflects 100.
  3. Internal Requisition:
    • Transfer 20 units from Main Warehouse -> Kitchen.
    • Verify: Warehouse = 80, Kitchen = 20.

Phase 2: The Sale (Front of House)

Goal: Process a customer order correctly.

  1. Log in as Cashier and open POS.
  2. Process Transaction:
    • Search for Test Burger.
    • Add Qty: 2 to cart.
    • Total should be 300.00.
    • Pay via Cash.
  3. Initial Receipt Check:
    • Verify printed receipt says [NOT COLLECTED].
  4. Background Check:
    • Kitchen stock for Test Burger should now be 18.

Phase 3: Production (KDS)

Goal: Kitchen receives and completes the order.

  1. Open KDS Screen (Chef View):
    • Locate new ticket for 2 x Test Burger.
    • Verify Timer has started (0m).
  2. Cooking Process:
    • Click START COOKING (Button turns green).
    • Click MARK READY.
    • Verify: Ticket disappears from active screen.

End-to-End UAT (Continued)

Phase 4: The Handoff (Pickup Screen)

Goal: Server collects food, system logs the chain of custody.

  1. Open Pickup Screen (Server View):
    • Verify Order Card is flashing.
    • Verify "Server Name" matches the Cashier.
  2. Validation Test:
    • Attempt to click CONFIRM without selecting a name.
    • Result: Button must be disabled.
  3. Process Collection:
    • Select Collector: Your Name.
    • Click CONFIRM.
    • Result: Order card disappears immediately.

Phase 5: Documentation (Receipts)

Goal: Verify double-printing and status updates.

  1. Modal Popup:
    • A popup should appear immediately after confirmation.
  2. Verify Copy 1 (Client):
    • Label: *** CLIENT COPY ***
    • Text: SERVED BY: [Name]
  3. Verify Copy 2 (Kitchen):
    • Label: *** KITCHEN COPY ***
    • Text: COLLECTED BY: [Name]
    • Verify Cut Line (✂) exists between copies.

Final Sign-Off Checklist

Complete this section to certify the system is ready for Go-Live.

KDS: Ticket appeared on screen and was legally closed?
