End-to-End User Acceptance Test

Objective: Validate the entire workflow from Inventory Setup -> Sales -> Kitchen -> Reporting.

Phase 1: Setup & Station Logic

Goal: Ensure stock history is tracking and work stations are enforced.

  1. Inventory History (Back Office):
    • Go to Inventory. Find an item (e.g., Coca Cola).
    • Click the History button.
    • Verify: A modal opens showing past movements (GRV, Sales, Transfers).
  2. POS Station Selection:
    • Log in as a Cashier/Waiter. Open POS.
    • Verify: "Select Work Station" modal appears automatically.
    • Select: Main Bar.
    • Verify: Top bar displays "POS - Main Bar".

Phase 2: Complex Sales & Payments

Goal: Test Mobile Money providers, Stock Deductions, and Payment Restrictions.

  1. Mobile Money Transaction:
    • Add 2 x Coca Cola to cart. Click Process Payment.
    • Select Method: Mobile Money.
    • Verify: Provider options (MTN, Airtel, Zamtel) appear.
    • Select Airtel. Enter Amount. Click Confirm.
    • Verify: Receipt prints showing "Paid via Mobile Money (Airtel)".
  2. "Pay Later" Restriction:
    • Create a new order. Select "Add to Tab (Pay Later)". Confirm.
    • Go to Open Tabs and click Recall on that order.
    • Try to checkout again by selecting "Add to Tab".
    • Verify: System blocks action (Error: "Cannot select Pay Later on Open Tab").
    • Settles the debt using Cash.

Phase 3: Automated Workflow (Dashboard)

Goal: Verify auto-requisitions and dashboard alerts.

  1. Critical Stock Trigger:
    • Sell enough of an item to drop stock below 3 units.
    • Navigate to Dashboard.
    • Verify: Red "CRITICAL STOCK ALERT" banner appears.
    • Verify: "Pending Requisitions" count has increased by 1 (Auto-generated).
  2. Action Center Drill-Down:
    • Click the "Unpaid Invoices" card on the Dashboard.
    • Verify: It redirects straight to the POS screen (not Inventory or Reports).

End-to-End UAT (Continued)

Phase 4: Production & Pickup (KDS)

Goal: Verify Kitchen Display logic and Audio notifications.

  1. KDS Filtering:
    • Place an order with 1 Food Item (Burger) and 1 Drink (Coke).
    • Check KDS Screen.
    • Verify: Only the Burger appears (Drinks are filtered out).
  2. Pickup Audio:
    • Mark order as "Ready" on KDS.
    • On Dashboard or Pickup Screen, wait 5 seconds.
    • Verify: "Ping" sound plays and red badge count updates.
  3. Collection & Exit:
    • On Pickup Screen, select "Collected By: [Name]". Confirm.
    • Click Back to POS (Top Right Button).
    • Verify: Redirects to POS, not Dashboard.

Phase 5: Manual Stock Control

Goal: Test manual transfers and stock adjustments.

  1. Manual Transfer Cycle:
    • Request: Go to Inventory -> Transfers. Request 5 units from Warehouse -> Bar.
    • Dispatch: Log in as Warehouse Manager. Click "Dispatch". (Status: In Transit).
    • Receive: Log in as Bar Manager. Click "Receive". (Status: Completed).
    • Verify: Bar stock increases by 5.
  2. Stock Adjustment (GRV/Damage):
    • Go to Inventory. Click Adjust on any item.
    • Enter -1 (Negative one). Reason: "Damaged".
    • Verify: Stock count drops by 1 immediately.

Phase 6: Admin & Shifts

Goal: Ensure cash control and security.

  1. End Shift Report:
    • On POS, click End Shift Report.
    • Verify: Report shows Total Cash vs Mobile Money.
    • Verify: Mobile Money is broken down by provider (e.g., Airtel: 50, MTN: 20).
  2. Audit Trail:
    • Go to Inventory -> Audit Trail (or History).
    • Filter by Today's Date.
    • Verify: You see entries for "Sale", "Transfer_Out", and "Adjustment".
